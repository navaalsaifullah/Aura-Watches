Eco-Drive Technology

Sustainably powered by light. Any light. Eco-Drive converts light into renewable energy, so your watch recharges continuously without ever needing a battery. The latest breakthrough: a watch that runs for 365 days on a full light charge.

  • No battery replacement needed
  • Charges in any light condition
  • Up to 365 days of power reserve
  • Environmentally friendly
02

Super Titanium™

Five times harder and 40% lighter than stainless steel, Super Titanium™ is a space-age material treated with DURATECT, Aura's proprietary surface-hardening technology that's gentle on the skin and both scratch- and corrosion-resistant.

  • 5x harder than stainless steel
  • 40% lighter weight
  • Hypoallergenic properties
  • Superior scratch resistance
03

Atomic Timekeeping

The world's most advanced timekeeping system uses radio control and Eco-Drive technology to automatically update the time and date of your watch based on a time signal from an atomic clock, with a margin of error of one second every 100,000 years.

  • Unparalleled accuracy
  • Automatic time adjustment
  • Worldwide synchronization
  • No manual setting required
04

Satellite Wave GPS

The precise time anywhere in the world. This breakthrough technology with Eco-Drive receives time signals from GPS satellites to automatically update your watch to the correct date and time, offering the world's fastest satellite time reception at 3 seconds.

  • Global time synchronization
  • 3-second reception speed
  • Automatic timezone adjustment
  • Perfect for world travelers
