Warranty Information

All watches purchased from Aura Watches come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. This warranty includes repairs or replacements for issues related to the watch movement, hands, and dial.

What is covered:
  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Watch movement malfunctions
  • Issues with hands and dial
What is not covered:
  • Damage due to misuse or accidents
  • Water exposure beyond the watch's water resistance rating
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Normal wear and tear, including scratches
  • Battery replacement for quartz watches

To initiate a warranty claim, please contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Aura Watch purchase. If you're not happy with your watch, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase.

Return Conditions:
  • Watch must be unworn and in original condition
  • All original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of purchase
